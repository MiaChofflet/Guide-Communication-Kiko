Make-up

The different uses

Make-up is the most important part of KIKO.

It allows you to feel good about yourself, to look good for yourself or to smooth out "flaws". It can also be used for occasions such as weddings, birthday parties and much more. You can shape it as you wish.

A few key words

Communication guides are synonymous with key words. In the world of make-up, English terms are used a lot, whether in French or in other languages. That's why we've thought of everything, so if you have a problem, just consult your guide and reinterpret your requests in your own way.

Several collections

Chere's no shortage of collections at kiko. From the Little Mermaid range to the Organic one. Kiko is particularly successful at innovating in the creation of new, ever more elaborate collections. So there's something for everyone. What's more, you'll find a new collection for each period of the year (summer, autumn, winter, spring),
